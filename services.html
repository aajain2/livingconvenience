Network

Every smart device in your home is only as reliable as the network underneath it. We design and deploy enterprise-grade wired and wireless networks built for the demands of a fully automated residence. Structured cabling, managed switches, commercial-grade access points, and dedicated VLANs that isolate your IoT devices from your personal traffic. Every cable run is tested, labeled, and documented. We also design, source, install, and configure your routing, switching, and wireless infrastructure end to end. You don't need to think about the equipment unless you want to. Wi-Fi 7, seamless roaming, full property coverage indoors and out.

Light

Architecture after dark
Light in layers, then layer in control

The right lighting transforms a home more than any other single system. We design layered lighting environments where color temperature, intensity, and shade position all respond to a single command, tuned for the task at hand: cooking, entertaining, reading, sleeping. Tunable white fixtures shift from cool and energizing in the morning to warm and restful at night. Dimmers are matched to every fixture so there is no hum, no flicker, just clean light at every level. Motorized shades work in concert with your lighting, responding to time of day, season, or a scene you define. Outdoor lighting is designed with the same care: pathways that guide you safely through transitions, landscape lighting that accentuates architectural details and textures, and full Dark Sky compliance where required. Start with a few rooms or do the entire property. Every phase connects to the master plan.

Sound

Invisible when idle, immersive when called
Music and video, your way

We design audio and video systems around how you actually live. Morning news in the kitchen, vinyl in the den, surround sound in the theater, ambient music on the patio. Distributed whole-home audio lets you play different sources in every zone or group them together for a single experience across the property. Speakers can be architectural and invisible, flush-mounted into ceilings and walls, or they can be statement pieces in a dedicated listening room. We handle everything from simple multi-room distribution to full theater builds with projection, acoustics, and hidden equipment. Video walls that tile a single source across multiple displays or run independent feeds on each screen. We don't tie you to a single manufacturer. We test the major platforms, explain the tradeoffs, and help you choose what fits your home and your listening habits.

Security

Beyond the alarm panel. Protection that thinks with you.

A complete security system is more than an alarm. We unify door stations, video surveillance, smart locks, gate access control, fire protection, and intrusion detection into one coordinated system. When you arrive home, your geofence can unlock the gate, light a path from the driveway through the house, disarm the alarm, and start your evening playlist. When you leave, a single button arms everything, locks every door, lowers the temperature, closes the shades, and confirms it is done. If a door is left open, you will know. We also address the layer most integrators ignore: cybersecurity. Your IoT devices, cameras, and access points are isolated and hardened so your home network is not a vulnerability. If you already work with a security company you trust, we coordinate with them to make sure everything connects.

Climate

Comfort that anticipates

Temperature, air, energy

Climate is the system you notice only when it fails. We design HVAC integration, smart thermostat programming, and zoned temperature control so every room in your home is exactly where you want it, when you want it. Systems respond to occupancy, time of day, season, and weather forecasts. Pair climate with your shading system and the house manages solar gain automatically, keeping rooms cool without running the AC. We also work with solar, battery storage, and EV charging infrastructure for clients building toward energy independence. Whether you are retrofitting a single thermostat or designing whole-home climate automation from the ground up, we make sure the system is invisible, efficient, and responsive.

Intelligence

One system, one mind

Where everything converges

This is the layer that ties every discipline together. A control system replaces the dozen apps on your phone with a single interface: a wall panel, a tabletop screen, a remote, or your phone. One app to manage every light, speaker, lock, camera, shade, and thermostat in your home. From there, we build coordinated scenes. Tap "Movie" and the projector wakes, the sources switch, the lights dim, the shades drop, and the temperature adjusts. Tap "Goodnight" and every door locks, the alarm arms, the lights fade, and the house confirms it is secure. Then comes the next layer: automation that responds to conditions. Time of day, season, weather, occupancy, your calendar. The system learns how you live and adapts accordingly. As AI moves from the cloud to the edge, with voice agents, predictive models, and on-device processing, the homes we build today are ready. Open protocols like Matter and Thread. Infrastructure with headroom. No rip and replace in three years.
